Our Mission

To create a drug-free Kerala by empowering individuals and communities through education, outreach, enforcement, and support systems for those affected by addiction.

Our Vision

A future where every citizen lives free from the dangers of drug addiction, supported by a community that values health, responsibility, and resilience.

Campaign Overview

The 'Say No To Drugs' campaign is a statewide initiative by the Kerala Police to reduce drug usage through a comprehensive approach involving schools, families, NGOs, and law enforcement. Our efforts include education drives, recovery support, digital outreach, and public events aimed at awareness and prevention.

Campaign Timeline

2023 - Foundation Phase

Initial research, partnerships, and pilot projects in key districts.

2024 - Expansion & Awareness

Launch of major campaigns in schools, TV, and social media.

2025 - Empowerment & Support

Community support networks, mobile outreach vans, and volunteer training programs activated statewide.
