Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if someone I know is using drugs?

Reach out to a trusted adult, teacher, or contact a helpline immediately. Don’t stay silent.

Is there a helpline for anonymous support?

Yes. Call our 24/7 confidential helpline: 1800-DRUGFREE for assistance.
